Commercial Area

Shopping Street

The commercial area in our housing society is a vibrant and thriving hub of activity. It is home to a variety of businesses, including grocery stores, clothing stores, restaurants, and cafes. The area is also home to a number of public amenities. The commercial area is conveniently located within the housing society, making it easy for residents to do their shopping and run errands. It is also a popular place for people to gather and socialize. On weekends, the area is often filled with people enjoying the sunshine and strolling along the shops.

Play Area

Family Park

The kids' park in our housing society is a great place for children to play and have fun. The park is located in a central location, making it easy for parents to keep an eye on their children. The park is also well-maintained, with clean and safe equipment. The park has a variety of equipment, including swings, slides, climbing structures, and a sandpit. There is also a shaded area where parents can relax while their children play. The park is open from sunrise to sunset. Children under the age of 12 must be accompanied by an adult. The park has a number of safety features, including fencing, soft ground, and shade. There are also a number of rules and regulations in place to ensure the safety of all park users. The park is maintained by the housing society's management team. The team regularly cleans and repairs the equipment and ensures that the park is safe for children to play in.

Security Service

24/7 Security

The security of our housing society is of paramount importance to us. We have a number of security measures in place to keep our residents safe, including 24/7 security guards patrolling the premises, CCTV cameras installed at strategic locations, an access control system at the main gate, well-lit pathways and parking areas, and regular security audits.
