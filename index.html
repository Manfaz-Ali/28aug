Shopping Street

Play Area

Family Park

The kids' park in our housing society is a great place for children to play and have fun. The park is located in a central location, making it easy for parents to keep an eye on their children. The park is also well-maintained, with clean and safe equipment. The park has a variety of equipment, including swings, slides, climbing structures, and a sandpit. There is also a shaded area where parents can relax while their children play. The park is open from sunrise to sunset. Children under the age of 12 must be accompanied by an adult. The park has a number of safety features, including fencing, soft ground, and shade. There are also a number of rules and regulations in place to ensure the safety of all park users. The park is maintained by the housing society's management team. The team regularly cleans and repairs the equipment and ensures that the park is safe for children to play in.
